Our UV Printing Method

With UV printing, you can print on almost any surface, absorbent or not... it turns out great.

That flexibility translates to hundreds of uses. Small yard and retail signs, custom printed whiteboards, outdoor safety signage. And with this new found ability to print on traditionally non-porous surfaces like Styrene or hard boards, customers are starting to pay attention.

High Print Qualities

UV printing allows for high-gloss levels, vibrant colors, an array of coating and printing that's crisp and has a nice finished look. That's because UV inks tend to sit on top of the printed surface, rather then being absorbed into it. The end result is sharper and crisper look.
